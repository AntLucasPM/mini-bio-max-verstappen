Max Emilian Verstappen is a Dutch racing driver born on September 30, 1997 (27 years old) in Hasselt, Belgium. He has been a Formula 1 driver since 2015, making his debut for the Toro Rosso team. He is a four-time Formula 1 champion in 2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024 and finished third in 2019 and 2020 with Red Bull Racing, the team he has been racing for since 2016.

Início no Kart

Max began his career in motorsport, competing in his first kart race at the age of 4. He began racing professionally in 2005, the year in which he won his first local championship. During his karting career, he competed against drivers such as George Russell and Charles Leclerc, with whom he had a strong rivalry.

By 2013, he had become one of the leading competitors in the sport, having accumulated victories in Dutch, Belgian, European, and world championships.

Categorias de Base

His rise to single-seaters came in October 2013, shortly after Max turned 16 and won six of the eight karting championships in which he competed. He drove a Manor Motorsport car at the Pembrey circuit.

He then did another test, this time with a Dallara F311 from Motopark Academy. In January 2014, his participation in the Florida Winter Series was confirmed. Shortly after, he signed with Van Amersfoort Racing to compete in Formula 3. In July, he raced in Formula 3 Masters for Motopark. In F3, the Dutchman won ten races, being the only one to win six in a row.
